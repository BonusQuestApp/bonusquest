# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: Feb 2026*

## Our Commitment

We don't track or use your data off device. Ever.

## Summary

- **No personal data collection** - We don't collect names, emails, or phone numbers
- **No servers** - Everything happens on your device
- **No tracking** - We have no analytics, telemetry, or user tracking

## What Information BonusQuest Stores

### On Your Device

1. **Bank Account Bonus Data** 
   - Information about when, what, and how much the bonus was
   - The name of the institution

### Off Your Device

1. **Bank Account Bonus Data**
   - Your data is synced with iCloud, to allow useage of the app on other devices, or if
     you are restoreing the app after reinstallation.

## What Information is Shared

None!

## What We DON'T Do

BonusQuest **never**:
- Collects personal information
- Tracks your location
- Stores data on servers
- Shares data with third parties
- Uses analytics or telemetry
- Requires registration

## Children's Privacy

BonusQuest is meant for users 17+, and does not knowingly collect information from children.
The app has no age verification because it collects no personal information from anyone.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy:
- The "Last updated" date will change
- No retroactive changes can affect data (since we don't collect any)

## Contact

BonusQuest is available for free. For privacy questions, you can open an issue on GitHub
